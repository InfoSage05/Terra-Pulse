This Authority has no role in the calculation or collection of the Local Property Tax (LPT).
For further information in relation to the LPT, contact the Revenue Commissioners at:
https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-us/customer-service-contact/local-property-tax-lpt.aspx

The Residential Property Price Register contains particulars of sale prices from 1st. January 2010 to date. There are no further particulars (e.g. floor area, number of rooms, etc.) of the properties available.
If you need further information for the purpose of the LPT contact the Revenue Commissioners at:
http://www.revenue.ie/en/tax/lpt/index.html
